month four

Month four wasn't easy.

We fought, we hurt each other,

and I won't pretend otherwise.

But even in the middle of all that —

I still thought about you every day.

Still wanted you to be okay.

Whatever happens between us,

I hope you're taking care of yourself.

I hope you're eating well, sleeping enough,

and letting yourself feel happy.

You deserve that. Always.

Not just when things are good.

I want there to be a month five.

I really do.

— still here, month four 🌙
